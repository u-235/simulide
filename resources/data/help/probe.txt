
PROBE HELP:

Hover probe over wires or pins to see voltages at any point of the circuit.

Right-click for context menu:

- Add to Plotter:
    Open Plotter in bottom panel and plots probe voltage.

- Remove from Plotter: 
    Remove probe from plotter.

- Add to oscope: 
    Open Oscilloscope in bottom panel and display probe voltage wave.

- Remove from oscope: 
    Remove Oscilloscope.
